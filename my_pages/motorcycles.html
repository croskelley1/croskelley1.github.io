My Motorcycles

I really like motorcycles. I have a couple, and will probably go through a couple more in the future. Below I talk about the two I currently have, as well as some others I've enjoyed riding..

1990 Suzuki RM 125

I bought this bike for $200 on kind of a whim. I'd been on a dirt bike exactly once before and had never actually driven one. My older brother saw it for sale while visiting a friend and told me if I didn't buy it he was going to. So I used half of my first paycheck of a new job and bought it. Had to put some work into it, but it runs alright nowadays. I'm hoping to take it out soon!

2007 Harley Davidson Nightster 1200

The first road bike I rode was the same year and model, just a 1500cc. It was awesome. So last year when I decided I wanted to buy a road bike of my own, I got the same make and model. It's really fun to ride, albeit a little uncomfortable. I'm planning on getting some taller handle bars and am currently remaking one of the seats I have for it. I don't think I'll keep this one forever, but it defintely has been a good starter bike.
